LIFE IN A YEAR

Life in a Year is a 2020 American romantic drama film directed by Mitja Okorn, from a screenplay by Jeffrey Addiss and Will Matthews. The film stars Jaden Smith, Cara Delevingne, Cuba Gooding Jr., and Nia Long. Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith serve as executive producers under their Overbrook Entertainment banner.Life in a Year was released on November 27, 2020, by Sony Pictures Releasing.

MAIN ACTORS

Jaden Smith as Daryn and Cara Delevingne as Isabelle.
