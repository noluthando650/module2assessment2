COMING TO AMERICA

Coming to America is a 1988 American romantic comedy film directed by John Landis and based on a story originally created by Eddie Murphy, who also stars in the lead role. The film also co-stars Arsenio Hall, James Earl Jones, Shari Headley, and John Amos. The film was released in the United States on June 29, 1988. Eddie Murphy plays Akeem Joffer, the crownprince of the fictional African nation of Zamunda, who travels to the United States in the hopes of finding a woman he can marry.

MAIN ACTORS

Eddie Murphy, Arsenio Hall and Shari Headley.
